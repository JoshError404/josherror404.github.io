Greek Origins

Chaos

Around 700 BC, the first written version of Greek Mythology was seen, the Theogony. The Theogony was written by the poet Hesiod and tells a story of the journey from nothingness (Chaos) to reality, and details a family tree of elements.

The Gods and Goddesses who evolved from Chaos:

  • Gaia (Earth)
  • Ouranos (Sky)
  • Pontos (Sea)
  • Tartaros (The Underworld)

Continued Story

Greeks believed that the earth was flat and that the center of the universe was Greece. More Gods and Goddesses were born later on including Zeus (god of the sky).

Greek Mythology has thirteen main gods

  • Zeus
  • Poseidon
  • Hera
  • Hephaestus
  • Dionysus
  • Athena
  • Artemis
  • Apollo
  • Ares
  • Demeter
  • Aphrodite
  • Hermes
